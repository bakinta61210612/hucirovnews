Beloved Dirt Racing Supporter Mike Kinneer Passes Away After Courageous Battle with Cancer

Today, on this Thanksgiving Day, we remember and honor the life of Mike Kinneer, a beloved member of the Nashport, Ohio community and an avid supporter of dirt track racing, who passed away after a courageous battle with cancer.

Mike Kinneer was more than just a staunch supporter of dirt racing. He was a devoted family man, a loving husband, father, brother, son, and a treasured friend to many. His passing leaves an irreplaceable void in the hearts of those who knew and loved him.

His wife Tracy Kinneer, sons CJ and Austin Kinneer, sister Cindy Kinneer Lawson, and the entire Kinneer family are in our thoughts and prayers during this difficult time. We also extend our condolences to Jamie Kinneer Curley, Karen Shinn, Sarah Anne, Hannah Curley, Jason Lawson, Jen Lawson Euler, and their families.

Mike’s fight against cancer was marked by strength, resilience, and an unyielding spirit that inspired all who knew him. His legacy will continue to live on in the memories of his loved ones and in the community he so passionately supported.

As we mourn his loss, we also celebrate his life and the impact he had on those around him. Mike Kinneer’s work here on earth may be done, but his spirit will continue to inspire and guide us. As we remember him today, we take comfort in the belief that he rests peacefully, free from pain and suffering, in his heavenly home.
