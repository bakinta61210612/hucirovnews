Maribel Guardia Shares Swimsuit Photo From &quot;Family Beach Trip&quot;

Maribel Guardia first rose to fame when she won Miss Costa Rica in 1978. She then began a career in the entertainment industry. Guardia starred in shows like Tu y yo and Una familia con suerte. Guardia is also a singer and TV host. She's currently enjoying a vacation in the Bahamas, and shared a set of swimsuit photos on Instagram. Guardia captioned the post, "AND THE BEAUTIFUL OF THIS IS LIFE. COSER SUENOS, BORDAR STORIES AND POWER TO UNLEASH. THE KNOTS OF OUR DAYS." How does she stay so fit? Read on to see 5 ways Maribel Guardia stays in shape and the photos that prove they work.

Guardia is an avid dancer. She shares a lot of posts on Instagram of herself dancing. Guardia shared this video of herself dancing on stage. "I try to carry healthy things in the bag, so I don't have to eat filthy things like chips, candy and so on."
