Everbridge App | Emergency Information

Downloading & installing the Everbridge app for Maroon Alert.

  • Search your app store for the Everbridge app. The app is published by Everbridge, Inc. and uses the following icon:

  • When you install and open the app, tap ”Find an organization or subscription”

  • Type “mississippi” in the search bar at the top of the screen.
    Next, tap the option with Maroon Alert:
    ​​​​​​​

  • Enter your MSU NetID & NetPassword to login:

  • Click “Done” for the app settings and approve any permissions the app may need.

  • The Feed screen will show any recent messages you have received.
    ​​​​​​​

  • Changing Weather Alert Subscriptions

    This section allows you to opt in to receive additional weather alerts. If you are receiving too many alerts, check this section to see what alerts are selected.

    Accessing from the App:

    Open the Everbridge App Tap the menu bar (three horizontal lines) Tap "Profile" and tap your name in the Accounts section. Select "Manage My Profile" Scroll down and tap "Edit" in the My Subscriptions section. Tap the triangle beside "Weather" to expand the weather menu. Do the same to expand the other weather options. Only select the weather alerts you wish to be informed of.

    Please note: some alerts, such as hail and lightning will result in many notifications. Tap Save.

    Accessing from the web:

    Login to https://my.msstate.edu Chose "Banner" on the left side Choose "Personal Information" Choose "Update Your Maroon Alert Information" Click on "Click here to go to the Maroon Alert Notification Portal" (under Step 3 on the page). This will take you to the "Maroon Alert" Everbridge portal. Edit "My Subscriptions" Click the triangle beside "Weather" to expand the weather menu. Do the same to expand the other weather options. Only select the weather alerts you wish to be informed of.

    Please note: Some alerts, such as hail and lightning will result in many notifications. Click Save. Log out of MyMsstate.

    •  

    Questions?

    Contact the Service Desk if you need assistance downloading or installing the app.
