Olivia Newton-John's Daughter Chloe Lattanzi Is Unrecognizable: Pics

Transformation alert! When Olivia Newton-John hit the red carpet in Las Vegas on Saturday, Aug. 8, she brought along her daughter, Chloe Lattanzi. But Chloe, who is Newton-John’s daughter with ex-husband Matt Lattanzi, looked nothing like she has in previous red carpet appearances with her famous mom.

Clad in a tiny embellished crop top and bandage skirt, Lattanzi, 29, was showing off plenty of skin in addition to her now-blonde locks. The aspiring singer and actress attended the event with the Grease star, 66, to celebrate the 35th anniversary of Xanadu and the premiere of their music video for the song “You Have to Believe,” a remix of Newton-John’s 1980 hit, “Magic.”
