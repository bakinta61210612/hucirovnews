Dax Shepard Helps Pregnant Kristen Bell in Adorable Samsung Holiday Ad

Dax Shepard and Kristen Bell are obsessed with Christmas, like, really obsessed. The couple, who star together in an adorable Samsung holiday commercial, tell Us Weekly they're complete "holiday junkies."

In the commercial, which Us has an exclusive first look at, the Parenthood actor, 39, and Veronica Mars alum, 34, emulate their real life and don matching Christmas ensembles and adorn their home with everything from fake snow to popcorn garlands. 

"Being in the holiday campaign is even better because we are both complete and utter holiday junkies," Bell, who is expecting her second child, told Us. "Any excuse we have to celebrate a little more, it feels very natural to us! We're just showing people a snapshot of our real life."

And they're not kidding — Shepard says they even wear matching sweaters in their own home.

"It's literally our favorite season, our favorite time of the year," the funnyman said. "We start with wearing matching Christmas sweaters the day after Halloween. And matching for the entire family, so not only us but that includes our daughter [Lincoln] and the canines too. And again that's not bulls–t because you can go on our Twitter and see three years ago us fully clad in matching outfits with the dogs," he added.
