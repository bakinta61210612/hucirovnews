Odette Annable Gives Birth, Welcomes Daughter Charlie With Dave: Photo

The stork has made its delivery! Odette Annable and her husband, Dave Annable, have welcomed a beautiful baby girl, the proud first-time mom announced via Instagram on Thursday, Sept. 10.

"Dave and I are thrilled to introduce you to the newest member of the Annable family…" Astronaut Wives actress, 30, shared with fans, alongside a sweet snap of her newborn daughter's hand wrapped around daddy's finger. "Charlie Mae Annable. Born September 7th. I took Labor Day quite literally this year. Dave gave me this blanket on Mother's Day and baby Charlie and I slept with it our entire hospital stay. Charlie is a lucky lady to have you as a papa, we love you so much @dave_annable. Thank you all for the love that you've shown us through this journey."

The Annables fittingly announced that they were expecting their first child together on Mother's Day. The couple discovered at a gender reveal party the following month that they were having a little girl.

"I was pulling boy all the way and then I heard 'girl' and I don't think I've ever felt more love in my heart. In that moment I was just, 'Oh my God!' It was just great," Dave, 35, told Us Weekly last month in an exclusive interview. "To have all of our family and friends and to be able to do it like that where you find out all together and on Odi's birthday – it was pretty special. You can't duplicate that ever again. It was pretty amazing for us."

The Brothers and Sisters alum, 35, told Us that he had lots of fun activities in store for his daughter — namely, sports. "My sweet little girl has got no choice," he joked. "I think definitely golf is a part of our family and Odi and I love to play, so I think we will bring our little girl out there and teach her. I grew up playing with my father and grandfather."

In early August, the pregnant actress celebrated the upcoming arrival of baby Charlie with a glamorous baby shower, shared exclusively with Us.
