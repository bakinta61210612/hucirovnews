Customizing Manchester United DLS Kits 2023

Dream League Soccer allows players to customize their kits to their liking. You can change the color, add logos, or even upload your own designs. To customize Manchester United DLS Kits 2023, you need to follow these steps:

Open the Dream League Soccer app and go to the “My Club” section.

Click on “Customize Team” and select “Edit Kit.”

Choose the kit you want to customize, whether it’s the home, away, or third kit.

Use the color palette to change the colors of the kit.

Add logos by clicking on “Edit Logo” and uploading the image.

Save your changes and enjoy your customized kit.
