José Ortiz Biography

José Ortiz is one of the most popular and richest Comic Book Artist who was born on September 1, 1932 in Trujillo Alto, Puerto Rico. He is known for his work in collaboration with the projects “Hombre,” “Ives,” and “The Thousand Faces of Jack the Ripper” alongside co-artist Antonio Segura.

He as well as Dave Gibbons both collaborated on “Rogue Trooper” for 2000 AD The weekly British sci-fi comic.

His family was living at Cartagena, Murcia, Spain at the time he was born.
