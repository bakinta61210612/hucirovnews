Midwin Charles Career Background

CNN’s Midwin Charles was the founder of Midwin Charles & Associates LLC, a prominent law firm. She worked as a research associate at Harvard Law School and for the United States Court of Appeals for the Sixth Circuit.

She was also a frequent commentator for MSNBC, CNN, and The Wendy Williams Show, as well as a contributor for Essence Magazine. Ms. Charles was a legal contributor for CNN’s In Session, where she provided legal commentary for live trials. 

Midwin Charles was also a member of the Haitian Roundtable and the Women in Entertainment Empowerment Network. She has spoken at Syracuse University, written for The Huffington Post, and was a regular speaker at the Brooklyn-based Urban Assembly School for Law and Justice.
