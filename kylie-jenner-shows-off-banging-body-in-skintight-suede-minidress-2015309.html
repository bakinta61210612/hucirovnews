Kylie Jenner Shows Off Banging Body in Skintight Suede Minidress

Dayum! Kylie Jenner strutted her curvaceous bod in a skintight, sexy dress while out for lunch with Tyga in L.A. on Tuesday, Sept. 29.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 18, rocked the camel-colored bodycon creation by Mistress Rocks with matching Alexandre Birman sandals and a tan Balenciaga handbag. Jenner sported her jet black hair in French pigtail braids.

The youngest of the Kardashian-Jenner crew loved her fall look, as evidenced by the four Instagram photos and videos she posted. She seemed especially excited about her makeup, crediting celeb artist Hrush Achemyan with her smoky cat-eye, which matched her dress. In one video, she thanked the makeup artist by saying, “Come live with me Hrush!!!!!”
