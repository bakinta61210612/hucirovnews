Daisy Edgar-Jones is a British actress who is currently popular for her role in the Netflix drama series, Normal People. She has made appeared on several TV shows.

Daisy Edgar-Jones Bio & Family: Wiki, Age, Parents, Education

The 22-years-of-age actress was born with the name Daisy Jessica Edgar-Jones on 24 May 1998, in Islington, London, England. She is the daughter of her parents, Philip Edgar-Jones and Wendy-Edgar Jones. Her Scottish father, Philip, is the director of Sky Arts, and one of the creators of Big Brother. Besides, her Northern-Irish mother, Wendy, worked as an editor on TV dramas.

As per the wiki sources, Daisy grew up in Muswell Hill, London, and later attended The Mount, Mill Hill International for her education. She later enrolled at Woodhouse College before joining the National Youth Theatre. Daisy also studied with the Open University. She possesses white ethnicity and her birth sign is Gemini.

How Tall Is Daisy Edgar-Jones? Her Height & Body Measurements

Daisy stands tall at a height of 5 feet and 6½ inches (1.69 meters) and has a body weight of 114.5 pounds (52 kg). Her body measurements read at 34-26-34 inches. Besides, she has dark brown hair and dark brown eyes.

Her Career In Movies & TV Shows, Net Worth

Daisy began her acting career with a Christmas special role in the British sitcom, Outlander, in 2016. The same year, she landed the recurring role of Olivia Marsden in the British comedy-drama series, Cold Feet, where she appeared till 2020. She booked the main role of Emily Gresham in the 2019 series, War of the Worlds. Daisy recently appeared as Marianne Sheridan in the Netflix Irish drama series, Normal People.

As of her big-screen performance, Daisy played the role of Cassie in the 2018 drama movie, Pond Life. Her professional net worth is yet to be disclosed on media sources.

Is Daisy Dating Anyone?

The young actress is currently dating 29-year-old British actor Tom Varey. The two met each other on the set of Indie film Pond Lake, back in 2018.
