Five of Our Favorite Kathy Wakile Tweets

She’s a housewife from Jersey. She is the wife of a hilarious businessman who always makes us laugh when he has some camera time. She is the mother of two seemingly intelligent and fun kids. She’s a killer in the kitchen. She is the cousin of Teresa Giudice. She is Kathy Wakile, and she has long since been one of our favorite housewives. She has a way of saying what is on everyone’s minds with appropriateness and tact, and she never fails to make us smile. She is the epitome of a mom, always in the kitchen trying to make sure her loved ones are fed, and she’s always looking out for everyone’s best interests. We might not see much of her this season – so far – in Jersey, but she’s got plenty of interesting things to discuss with us on Twitter; and we keep up.
