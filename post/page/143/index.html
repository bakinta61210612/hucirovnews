As the United States’ first nationwide three-digit mental health crisis hotline goes live on Saturday, experts have called it “one of the most exciting things that has happened” in mental health care. As opposed to dialing a long 1-800 number, now people in distress can dial 9-8-8. It’s faster, easier to remember, and they’ll speak with trained mental health counselors.
