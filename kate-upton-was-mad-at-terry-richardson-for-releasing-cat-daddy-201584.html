Kate Upton Was Mad at Terry Richardson for Releasing &quot;Cat Daddy&quot;

Mad daddy. Kate Upton told Vogue UK in its June 2015 issue that she was annoyed with fashion photographer Terry Richardson for releasing her career-launching "Cat Daddy" video without her consent.

The 2012 video, which featured a bikini-clad Upton playfully executing the dance, catapulted the little-known model into the spotlight.

In mere days, the video — along with the "Cat Daddy" dance itself — went viral, attracting 21 million-plus hits on YouTube since its release. Despite the instant success of the behind-the-scenes clip, Upton, 22, was not happy at the time with Richardson, 49.

"I was like, 'That was disrespectful, you could have told me!'" Upton recalled of her conversation with the controversial photog.

Kate Upton- Cat Daddy from Terry Richardson on Vimeo.

Three years — and two Sports Illustrated Swimsuit issue covers — later, Upton has let go of her grudge. "Now, obviously, it's fine," she noted.
