Bella Thorne Turns 18, Shuts Down Six Flags Theme Park for Celebration

A birthday to remember! Bella Thorne had a party to remember on Saturday, Oct. 10, when she shut down Six Flags Magic Mountain in California, to celebrate turning 18.

The Shake It Up alum was joined by her boyfriend, Gregg Sulkin, for the lavish celebrations, which started off with a dinner aboard a private yacht, followed by an opportunity to blow out the candles on a three-tier birthday cake.

"The ladies of the night," an excited Thorne captioned a photo of herself (dressed in a stunning bright red, strapless dress) and her bevvy of gorgeous girlfriends who posed for a pre-party photograph. She also shared an Instagram video of her birthday serenade and some very sultry candle blowing too.

Later in the evening, Thorne, and her buddies who included Olivia Holt, Alexa Yarness, Peyton List, and Halston Sage, then changed into more suitable theme-park attire (jeans, Henri Bendel backpack, and a Happy Birthday Bitch sweatshirt) to have unlimited fun on the Six Flags theme park rides.

"Shut down Six Flags last night like……. @sixflagsmagicmountain @wildfoxcouture," she captioned a shot of her and her pals getting ready to let loose on the rides.

Thorne's weekend festivities are just the latest in a string of birthday celebrations for the DUFF actress. On Thursday, Oct. 8, her actual birthday, she smooched her way through dinner and desert with her boyfriend Sulkin.

The next day she took to the skies with her beau, for her first flying lesson. "On cloud 9 … Had my first flying lesson today @greggsulkin," she captioned a snap of her standing by the plane.
