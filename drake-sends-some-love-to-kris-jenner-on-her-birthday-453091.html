Drake Sends Some Love To Kris Jenner On Her Birthday!

Even though he and Kanye West are not on very good terms, Drake still made sure to send his mother in law, Kris Jenner, some birthday love! Check out his sweet message!

As fans of Keeping Up With The Kardashians probably know by now, yesterday, the famous momager turned 65 years old and she was, undoubtedly showered with love by all of her family members.

Friends and followers did the same as well, celebrating Kris on social media and Drake was one of them!

The rapper took to his IG Stories to mark Kris Jenner’s milestone birthday, reposting a pic of the matriarch rocking a white power suit.

Alongside it he, wrote: ‘Happy birthday love always [Kris Jenner].’

This is no surprise since Drake has always had a pretty close relationship with the KarJenner clan.

Actually, not too long after Kylie Jenner’s split from her baby daddy, Travis Scott, she was even rumored to be dating Drake!

While the two did seem to be getting pretty flirty at a party around that time, things didn’t really go anywhere romantically but they are definitely still friends!

On the other hand, Drake has a pretty complicated and long history with Kanye West!

The two have been friends on and off throughout more than a decade, going through a lot of feuds and clapping at one another several times, including harsh disses.

From Kanye’s point of view, Drake has made a great ‘sparring partner’ and a fellow artist that always motivates him to ‘go to the studio.’

But Drake definitely did not see it as just healthy competition when Drake’s rival Pusha T. revealed Drake had a secret son a couple of years ago.
