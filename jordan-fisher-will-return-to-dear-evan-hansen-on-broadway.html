Jordan Fisher Will Return To Dear Evan Hansen On Broadway Celeb Secrets

Get ready theatre fans! Jordan Fisher will be returning to the Tony award-winning Broadway production of Dear Evan Hansen which reopens on December 11th.

On Tuesday morning (June 1), Fisher and his co-stars officially announced their return with an electric performance of “You Will Be Found” on Good Morning America. Some of the stars accompanying him in December include Jessica Phillips (Heidi Hansen), Christiane Noll (Cynthia Murphy), and Gabriella Carrubba (Zoe Murphy).

https://youtu.be/jFQWE0BG_qo

After the performance, Fisher went to Twitter to share his emotions about the news. He said, “that was by far the most deeply meaningful live performance of my career. I’m in tears. So happy.”

Fisher originally joined the cast in the title role in January 2020. As a result of the pandemic, his stint in the show was cut short only two months later.

Dear Evan Hansen follows the story of a high school student who finds himself in a situation that blows out of proportion.

Before the Broadway musical reopens, the film adaptation of Dear Evan Hansen will release in theaters on September 24. A couple of names you might recognize in the film include Ben Platt, Julianne Moore, Amy Adams, and Kaitlyn Dever.
