Olivia Rodrigo Drops New Teaser And Deletes All Instagram Photos! Celeb Secrets

Time to grab your keys and get ready to blast sad music through the suburbs again, Olivia Rodrigo just dropped a teaser on her Instagram and we can’t help but feel like she’s finally coming out with more music!

The singer/songwriter cleared out her entire Instagram page and uploaded an uncaptioned video of an old-style television with ice cream melting on the screen. Along with the Instagram clean-out, she also revamped her website with the same visual. But what does it mean, Olivia?!

We’ve got our detective hats on trying to figure out what the artist is releasing soon.

Just before she cleared out her Instagram page, Olivia uploaded photos of her and “Heather” singer, Conan Gray, listening to Taylor Swift‘s newest track, “You All Over Me,” with the caption: “Conan and Olivia listening to “you all over me” but it’s a renaissance painting.”

This isn’t the first time Gray and Rodrigo have hung out! Rodrigo posted a few weeks ago on her Instagram stories a screenshot of a text from Gray telling her she “left two pistachios in the studio.”

Excuse me, studio?! Could we be getting a collab from the duo soon?? Is Rodrigo releasing an album with a feature from Gray? We need answers!

Rodrigo’s debut single, “drivers license,” has made record-breaking moves, but now we’re ready to have more songs from her on repeat!

We’ll be sure to keep up on all things Olivia Rodrigo as her success continues to grow, so keep a lookout for more updates on new content from us of Olivia.
