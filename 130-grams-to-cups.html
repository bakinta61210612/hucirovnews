Simple ways to convert 130 grams to cups

Use the conversion formula

When converting units of measure, it is important to use the conversion formula. This will ensure accuracy when converting between different units.  Here is the conversion equation:

cups = m / (ρ * cup size) in which:
  • cup size: the different size in the cooking or baking like US customary cup (236,6 ml), US legal cup (240 ml), metric cup (250 ml), Imperial cups (284 ml),…
  • ρ: the density of ingredients (g/ml or g/cm3)
  • m: the weight of ingredients (g)
