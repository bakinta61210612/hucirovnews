Laurie Bonnell Age 47 Died: St. Johns, NL Community Mourns

It is with profound sadness that we report the passing of Laurie Bonnell, a beloved mother and esteemed member of the St. John’s, Newfoundland and Labrador community. Known for her warmth, kindness, and unwavering love for her family and community, Laurie’s loss has left those who knew her grappling with an immense void.

A Life Well-Lived

Laurie Bonnell was a beacon of positivity, illuminating the lives of those around her with her radiant smile and infectious laughter. Her spirit, resilience, and generosity were unmatched, leaving a lasting impression on everyone she met. She was a true embodiment of love and compassion, always ready to lend a helping hand or a listening ear.

A Mother’s Love

Most significantly, Laurie was a loving mother. Her children were her world, and she dedicated her life to nurturing, supporting, and empowering them. She was their greatest cheerleader, always encouraging them to pursue their dreams and stand up for what they believed in.

Her love extended beyond her own children, as she often played a maternal role within the broader community, offering guidance and support to those who needed it.

A Pillar of the Community

Laurie’s impact on St. John’s was monumental. She was deeply involved in various community initiatives, consistently demonstrating her commitment to making a positive difference. Her tireless dedication, coupled with her passion for service, made her a cherished community figure.

She believed in the power of unity and regularly encouraged others to engage in community service. Her efforts have left a lasting legacy, inspiring many to continue her work and uphold the values she held dear.

Remembering Laurie

As we mourn Laurie’s loss, we also celebrate her life – a life characterized by love, service, and an unwavering commitment to family and community. Her memory lives on in the hearts of those she touched, serving as a gentle reminder of the difference one person can make. A Gofundme page has been created to support her last ride & we request all the supporters to contribute in whatever manner is possible so that we can say heartly goodbye.

Our deepest condolences go out to Laurie’s family during this difficult time. May they find solace in the love and support of the community she loved so dearly, and may her spirit continue to guide and inspire them.
