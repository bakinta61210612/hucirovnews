Where Does MrBeast Live?

MrBeast is one of the YouTubers with the most subscribers on the video-sharing platform. People know him for his stunt videos and his generosity, but there’s something no one seems to know about him. Where does MrBeast live?

MrBeast lives in Greenville, North Carolina. This is the same place where he grew up alongside his older brother, CJ Donaldson (known online as MrBro).

MrBeast never thought that making YouTube videos for fun since an early age would lead him to become such an important person in showbusiness.

Who’s MrBeast?

Jimmy Donaldson, also known online as MrBeast, is a YouTuber, entrepreneur, and philanthropist. He is part of a community of American YouTubers that people love for their fun content and philanthropic acts.

MrBeast began posting videos on the YouTube platform at the age of 13 under the pseudonym “MrBeast6000.” His first videos consisted of Let’s Plays and videos where he estimated the wealth of other YouTubers.

Finally, in 2017, he went viral after posting a video titled “Counting to 100,000” which garnered tens of thousands of views in just a few days.

After the popularity of his channel continued to grow, MrBeast began to include expensive challenge videos. These included thousands of dollars in rewards and even videos donating large amounts of money.

MrBeast hired his childhood friends to be part of his growing new brand. Today, his team comprises 30 people of diverse backgrounds.

During the Pewdipie vs T-Series campaign to see who would become the YouTube channel with the most subscribers, MrBeast had unusual participation. 

He bought printed and digital advertising, and numerous radio advertisements to help Pewdiepie gain more subscribers. At the Super Bowl LIII, he bought seats for him and his friends to wear shirts that said “Sub 2 Pewdiepie.”

In January 2021, MrBeast surpassed the 50 million subscriber mark on YouTube, a very significant figure within the community. Additionally, MrBeast has other YouTube channels including MrBeast Gaming and MrBeast Shorts.

His Philanthropic Approach to Stardom

By 2020, MrBeast was one of the highest-paid YouTubers on the platform. It’s all thanks to the channel’s millions of views and also due to funding and sponsorship he gets from the coupon company Honey.

With all his money, MrBeast usually posts videos giving away large sums of money. Donating $10,000 to local Los Angeles animal shelters or $100,000 worth of items to homeless shelters are some of his most typical videos. 

These types of videos have a lot of impact on his YouTube channel.

In 2019, MrBeast and YouTuber Mark Rober organized a fundraising event called #TeamTrees.  The goal of the project was to raise $20 million for the Arbor Day Foundation and in return, the organization would plant a tree for every dollar raised.

A few months after that, MrBeast announced that he would launch a virtual restaurant called MrBeast Burger. The restaurant will launch in around 300 locations across the United States and will even have an app.

Popular YouTuber TheReportOfTheWeek reviewed MrBeast Burger, giving the MrBeast franchise a score of 7 out of 10. This makes the burgers a must-try treat for all.

Best Online Creator

Over time, MrBeast became one of the most important and recognized content creators on the entire YouTube platform, all due to its exponential growth and vast amount of views.

MrBeast is credited with pioneering various types of content found on YouTube, such as “challenge videos” and “expensive stunts”. This marked a very important milestone in the way content creators made videos.

MrBeast’s philanthropist projects earned him recognition among followers due to his giving nature. He worked side by side with various foundations to make many people happy throughout the country and the world.

For all the above, MrBeast remains in the highest places of popularity. It’s pretty unlikely for his fame to diminish in the near future.

Where Will He Go Next?

With the new “MrBeast Burger’s” franchise, MrBeast hopes to reach the homes of thousands of families. The goal is to make its channel’s name and its content more recognized even by people who do not use the platform.

MrBeast will continue to make fun videos for his subscribers, which is a very important action for his channel.  And, in the future, he hopes to continue to maintain his position as one of the most representative YouTubers in the industry.
