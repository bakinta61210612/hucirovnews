Mississippi Rent Assistance

Rental Assistance for Mississippians Program, (RAMP), provides rental assistance for eligible Mississippians who are in financial need. Renters who are eligible for benefits must have a member of their household that is experiencing homelessness, or in need of shelter, rapid-rehousing ,, or any other services related homelessness.
