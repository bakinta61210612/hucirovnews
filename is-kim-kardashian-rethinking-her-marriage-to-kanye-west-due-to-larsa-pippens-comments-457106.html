Is Kim Kardashian Rethinking Her Marriage To Kanye West Due To Larsa Pippens Comments?

Is Kim Kardashian rethinking her marriage to Kanye West due to Larsa Pippen’s comments? Once upon a time, Larsa Pippen and Kim Kardashian were best friends, after the two stopped following each other on social media, rumors surrounded the ex-BFFs about the cause of their split. No one knows exactly what happened, but due to an interview Larsa recently gave to Hollywood Raw, the public has a better idea of what transpired and its name is Kanye West. Larsa Pippen spilled the tea and dropped some major bombshells including that she believes Kanye West has brainwashed everyone — including Kim!

Now, the U.K. publication “New” is addressing the fallout and quoted a source who said that Kim isn’t quickly jumping to defend Kanye from Larsa’s accusations, but rather is rethinking her marriage and is tired of losing friends due to Kanye West’s behavior, including Chrissy Teigen and John Legend.

An insider spoke to the publication and stated the following in the November 23, 2020, issue.

Larsa’s comments have been a bitter pill to swallow, but they’ve also been the wake-up call Kim needs to start making changes. Kim wants to ensure she doesn’t lose any more friends, and that she is standing her ground with Kanye more to show her independence without rocking the boat.

Kim and Kanye have been dodging divorce rumors since he announced his run for president and then made troubling statements about Kim and his oldest child, North West, during his campaign rally. Kanye, who is living with bipolar disorder, continued with bizarre behavior until Kim Kardashian ultimately addressed his mental health condition on her official Instagram account.
