What does being made mean in Goodfellas?

In the American and Sicilian Mafia, a made man is a fully initiated member of the Mafia. To become “made”, an associate first must be Italian or of Italian descent and sponsored by another made man. Is a capo a made man?

A caporegime or capodecina, usually shortened to capo or informally referred to as “captain”, is a rank used in the Mafia (both the Sicilian Mafia and Italian-American Mafia) for a made member of an Italian crime family who heads a “crew” of soldiers and has major social status and influence in the organization.
