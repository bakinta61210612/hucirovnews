Jay Sean Welcomes Baby Girl Ayva With Wife Thara Natalie: First Photo

Jay Sean has a new little lady in his life — his daughter! The R&B crooner and his wife, Thara Natalie, welcomed a beautiful baby girl on Friday, Dec. 13, he announced via Instagram on Dec. 16.

"Yuuuuup! I'm a daddy!!!!!" he wrote alongside a photo collage of his precious bundle of joy. "Meet our lil baby girl, Ayva. She was born on the 13th [of] December, weighing in at 6 pounds, 7 ounces."

Sean, 32, went on to describe his daughter's birth. "It was the most incredible moment of my life seeing her enter this world," he added. "Wifey @tharanatalie (who was unbelievably strong and composed thru it all btw — yogamommy!!) had a music playlist for her delivery, a mixture of diff artists and some of her fav songs (including mine)."

"Would u believe of all the songs on the playlist she was born to daddy's song!!?!!!!" he continued. "She was born to 'Luckiest Man.' And of course — that I am!"
