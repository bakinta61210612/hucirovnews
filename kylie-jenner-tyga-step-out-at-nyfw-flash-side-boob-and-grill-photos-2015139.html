Kylie Jenner, Tyga Step Out at NYFW, Flash Side Boob and Grill: Photos

The look of love! Kylie Jenner and boyfriend Tyga stepped out on Saturday, Sept. 12, for Alexander Wang’s much-anticipated NYFW fashion show, and the young couple proved they were pros in the spotlight.

Jenner, 18, showed off her mile-long legs in a tight black long-sleeve dress and ankle-high platform boots. She wore her blonde tresses down and relaxed.

Meanwhile, Tyga, 25, flashed his new gold grills for cameras in an all-tan outfit and plenty of accessories.

The pair sat front-row with fellow It Couple Bella Hadid and The Weeknd (Us Weekly exclusively confirmed that the pair were an item back in May).

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star and the “Rack City” rapper have been spending plenty of time together. The duo turned up at the MTV VMAs last month after spending time in St. Barts with the entire Kardashian-Jenner clan and in Mexico for Jenner’s 18th birthday.
