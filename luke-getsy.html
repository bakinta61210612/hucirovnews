Luke Getsy Biography

Luke Getsy is one of the most popular and richest American Football Player who was born on February 16, 1984 in Munhall, Pennsylvania, United States. Getsy was appointed offensive coordinator for the Mississippi State Bulldogs on December 31, 2017.

He was appointed the Green Bay Packers’ offensive quality control coach on February 7, 2014. For the 2016 NFL season, he was named the Packers’ Wide Receivers coach.

In 2009, Getsy was appointed the West Virginia Wesleyan College’s offensive coordinator, quarterbacks coach, and academic coordinator. He was appointed as the quarterbacks coach and offensive coordinator at Indiana University of Pennsylvania in 2011.

On January 21, 2019 Getsy was named the quarterbacks coach to the Green Bay Packers. On March 12, 2020, He was promoted to quarterbacks coach/passing game coordinator.
