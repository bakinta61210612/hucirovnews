Robert Pattinson News - Us Weekly

Name:Robert Pattinson

Hometown:London, England

Relationship:Single

Birthday:May 12, 1986

About

After making his film debut as Cedric Diggory in 2005’s Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire, Robert Pattinson took the world by storm as vampire heartthrob Edward Cullen in the 2008 film adaptation of Stephenie Meyer’s bestselling book Twilight.

Pattinson went on to star in five Twilight Saga films alongside Kristen Stewart. He branched out from the franchise with roles in Remember Me (2010), Water for Elephants (2011), The Lost City of Z (2016), Good Time (2017), The Lighthouse (2019) and Tenet (2020). The actor also played Bruce Wayne in the 2022 superhero film The Batman.

Pattinson dated Stewart on and off from 2009 to 2013. He moved on with FKA Twigs in 2014, proposing one year later, but the pair called it quits in 2017. Pattinson began dating Suki Waterhouse in 2018. The couple are expecting their first baby.
