Mindy Kaling Sets the Record Straight About Book With Ex B.J. Novak

The Mindy and B.J. Project is not quite what fans think. Mindy Kaling took to Twitter and Instagram on Tuesday, May 26, to set the record straight on the upcoming book she is working on with her ex-boyfriend B.J. Novak.

The FOX-turned-Hulu star, 35, shot down rumors that she and Novak, also 35, are writing a tell-all book about their relationship, but confirmed that something is in the works.

"Many of you asked about a book that my friend BJ and I are working on," Kaling wrote in an Instagram caption. "I heard somewhere it was a tell-all which I actually found exciting, because that sounds sexy and I grew up looking like a little fat boy." 

"But it's not a tell-all," the Mindy Project star continued. "What I CAN tell you it will be super cool and funny and even better than that. For now we are researching." 

"I swear I'm not trying to be mysterious! We are just starting work on it. Here is a cool pic of us working at my house," she added, referencing the shot she shared of children's book characters Frog and Toad.
