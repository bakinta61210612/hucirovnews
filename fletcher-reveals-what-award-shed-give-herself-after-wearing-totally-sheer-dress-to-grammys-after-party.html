Fletcher Reveals What Award Shed Give Herself After Wearing Totally Sheer Dress to Grammys After

Fletcher rocked what might be her most daring outfit to date, and she claimed an award for it!

The 28-year-old “Becky’s So Hot” hitmaker wore a totally sheer, mesh dress to Universal Music Group’s 2023 After Party after the 2023 Grammys on Sunday (February 5) in Los Angeles.

She paired the gray long-sleeved dress with a pair of knee-high black boots. While she wore a pair of black briefs under her dress, she opted out of wearing a bra, instead exposing her almost entirely bare chest.

Showing off her look on social media, Fletcher revealed what award she thought she’d win for the look.
