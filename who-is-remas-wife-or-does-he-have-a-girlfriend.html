Who Is Remas Wife or Does He Have a Girlfriend?

Rema is not married but the Afrobeat sensation has been linked to women such as Tems, Ayra Star, and Selena Gomez – an American actress and musician. Also, he had a romantic date with a lady named Nimmie.

Rema has publicly aired his view about love and relationship through his music. Yet, this does not seem to be the exact case when it comes to the revelation of the women he has been with. Whatever the case, the Calm Down crooner has had some form of connection with women within and outside the music industry.

Who is Rema’s Girlfriend?

From the information that is available, Rema does not have a girlfriend. However, around 2020, it was rumored that the singer was in a relationship with a lady named Niimie. Also, sometime in his career, there were rumors claiming that the singer may be married.

However, Nimmie claimed to not have been in any relationship with the Mavin Records signee. Instead, she was a fan whom he took out for a romantic date and other hangouts.

Also, it turned out that the news suggesting that he could be married was false.

As far as we know, Rema has not publicly opened up about dating anyone. and in 2020,  he cried out about his needing a wife. This came following a video of a family doing a “woman challenged”. He gushed over the video on Instagram and revealed how he needed a wife to do videos like that with.

Fans were quick to remind him that he could marry Nimmie. Also, several suggestions came up.

Is Rema Married? 

Rema is not married and has claimed to be afraid of going into a relationship. According to him, a lot of women would want to be with him because of fantasy and not for who he is. As such, it will be difficult for these women to stick around when there is trouble or when he is going through challenges.

He further made it known that sometimes, he feels as though people like him were not meant to be married.

Going deeper into the conversation, the singer revealed that the internet is scary and there are just too many red flags. To him, relationships become too complicated even before you go into them. Well, this is the reason why Rema is not married and why he has not been in a relationship.

Like many other famous people, rather than chasing a love that may be difficult to find, he focuses on building a career. Thankfully,  his focus on his career has helped in making him one of the most celebrated Nigerian musicians. His song Calm Down which he made a remix with Selena Gomez became the most-viewed video in the history of Afrobeat.

He revealed in a 2022 interview that he was not dating anyone and was not even trying to. Rema has also added that even if he decided to be in a relationship, he would keep it out of social media because of the heavy negativity that lingers on the internet.

A Look at the Women Rema Has Been Linked to 

Rema was Rumored to be Dating Selena Gomez

31 year-old American singer and actress, Selena Gomez has been rumored to have great chemistry with Rema. This came as a result of their collaboration on Rema’s hit song Calm Down remix which was released in 2022.

Another contributing factor to why the suggestion that the two could be having an affair was when Rema shared a video of Selena singing Calm Down and captioned it “Love You”.

Fans had mixed reactions to the entire situation. While others congratulated them and hoped they would have a good love life, others thought it was only music. Others also shared their opinion that Selena was older and Rema may not be dating her.

As much as it is available, there is no evidence that the singers are in a relationship. However, it is undeniable that they have developed friendships and make comments on each other’s posts on social media.

Rema called Ayra Star, “The One”  

The supposed intimate relationship between Ayra Star and Rema has been among the trending topics in the Nigerian music industry. The Mavins Records members share a unique friendship and close ties which has further cemented the rumors of a possible relationship.

Rema, the Soundgasm singer took to his social media page to wish Ayra Star a happy birthday and referred to her as his love as per the caption: “More life my luv”. He had also referred to her as “The One” in an interview whilst praising her. Furthermore, they have been seen together at events and parties as well as casually posting about one another on their social media pages.

This may not be enough however to determine the certainty of an actual relationship going on between them. Rema has come out to emphasize that the Bloody Samaritan singer is only a close friend and fellow signee to the same label. He has also professed how he has a hard time going to places without her as she makes for good company.

There Were Suggestions that He Could Be Dating Tems 

Grammy Award winner, Temilade Openiyi popularly Tems is one of the famous women that have been linked to Rema.

It became a major story in Nigeria when Rema use an image of Tems as his display picture on Twitter in July 2022. Although he removed it after some days, fans could not unsee the gesture. Hence, it is widely believed that it is either they are romantically involved or Rema probably has a crush on the Try Me singer.

Rema has posted about Tems on social media on several occasions, praising her creativity and her talent. While the singers may not have given any insight into their relationship, they are evidently friends as they have been seen casually hanging out.

Rema Has Been Spotted with South African Celebrity, Tyla

Tyla Seethal is a South African singer and TikTok star signed to Epic records. Following her interview in February 2021 with Trending SA, she declared her interest in collaborating with Rema.

A year later, Rema was seen holding hands with Tyla in Los Angeles as they arrived at a club together. This got many anticipating a future music collaboration between the duo. A possible start-up of a love affair between the South African 2002-born singer and the Nigerian musician was also anticipated but did not materialize.

He has also been linked to the Dumebi Video Vixen, Diana Eneje

Born on 19 August 2002, Diana Eneje is a fashion model, social media influencer, and entrepreneur. She came into the limelight when she featured in Rema’s song Dumebi which was released in 2019 as a model and video vixen.  At the time of the song’s release, Diana was only 17 years old.

She is one of the very first ladies rumored to be in a relationship with Rema since his fame. After Diana was featured in the Dumebi video, a lot of speculation began over their possible involvement behind the scenes. Both celebrities however have not given any concrete response to the claims.

Professionally, the pair worked briefly as models for a clothing brand called Almost Angels in 2019 and were featured in HP’s X360 pavilion laptop advert together. Diana has also worked so far with brands such as Stanbic IBTC, Infinix, Krispy Kreme, Hewlett- Packard (HP).  The Dumebi video model won the 2017/2018 Nigerian Teens Choice Most influential/Fashionable Female award.

Despite being connected to all these women, Rema has shown a lot of dexterity in keeping his love life separate from his professional life unlike many of his counterparts in the industry.
